Railway Intrusion Detection Based on Machine Vision: A Survey, Challenges, and Perspectives

About

At present, the cause of serious impact on railroad safety is the illegal intrusion of foreign objects into the safety area of the railroad, the main foreign objects on both sides of the line are plastic sheds, dust nets, color steel sheds and other buildings, structures and plastic film, tin foil, colored steel tiles and other construction materials.

At present, railroad inspection is more than the use of manual or the use of integrated inspection vehicles for inspection, these traditional inspection methods not only have low detection efficiency, poor real-time, low detection accuracy, an inspection of blind areas and other problems, while some inspection tasks also exist a certain degree of danger.

Detection of foreign objects on electrified railways is difficult because of the diverse forms, random distribution, different sizes, short detection time and high response requirements for detection equipment, and environmental factors that can degrade detection accuracy. Therefore, the development of efficient, accurate and stable foreign body detection technology is essential to ensure railroad safety and operational efficiency
